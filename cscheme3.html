ATAL INNOVATION MISSION (AIM)

AIM Startup India Seed Fund

Provides financial assistance of up to INR 20 lakh to startups in the ideation and early validation stage. This scheme specifically encourages applications from women entrepreneurs and those working on women-centric solutions

Offers incubation support, mentorship, and market access to women-led startups across various sectors. The program provides a platform for networking, learning, and growth for aspiring women entrepreneurs.

AIM Women Innovators Network (WIN)

A nationwide network connecting women innovators and entrepreneurs, fostering collaboration, knowledge sharing, and mutual support. WIN provides a platform for members to learn from each other, access resources, and build lasting connections.

AIM State Entrepreneurship Missions (SEMs)

Each Indian state, under the AIM umbrella, has established SEMs providing localized support and resources for women entrepreneurs. Programs and initiatives vary by state, but typically offer funding, incubation facilities, mentorship, training, and networking opportunities.
