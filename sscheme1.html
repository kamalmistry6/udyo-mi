MINISTRY OF MICRO, SMALL, AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (MSME)

Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY)

PMMY, also known as MUDRA (Micro Units Development and Refinance Agency) Yojana, aims to provide financial support to small entrepreneurs. It categorizes loans into Shishu, Kishor, and Tarun, based on the stage of business, to encourage the development of micro-enterprises

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY)

Promotes financial inclusion by providing bank accounts, overdraft facilities, micro-insurance, and credit cards to women entrepreneurs. Enables efficient financial management and access to formal banking services.

Visit Schemes
Credit Guarantee Fund Scheme for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE)

PMEGP is a credit-linked subsidy program aimed at generating employment opportunities by providing financial assistance for setting up micro-enterprises in both manufacturing and service sectors

Visit Schemes
Technology Centre Systems Programme (TCSP)

TCSP focuses on establishing a network of Technology Centers across states to provide technological and business support, enhancing the competitiveness of micro, small, and medium enterprises

Visit Schemes
Udyogini Scheme

Udyogini is a scheme that provides loans to women entrepreneurs for setting up and running small businesses. It focuses on skill development and financial support.

Visit Schemes
Skill Upgradation and Quality Improvement Program (SUKI)

SUKI focuses on enhancing the skills of entrepreneurs through training and development programs, aiming to improve the quality of products and services offered by micro, small, and medium enterprises.
